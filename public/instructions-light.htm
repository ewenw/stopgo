Instructions Summary

Game starts in seconds.
  • You are about to be assigned a role: Red or Blue.
  • You will play 3 matches, and each match you will be randomly paired with a new player of the other role.
  • Red player goes first, knows the state of the world, and chooses STOP or GO.
  • Blue player goes second, does not know the state of the world, and chooses LEFT or RIGHT.
  • You will be remembered about how your payoff is computed in before you make your decision.

Stop/Go Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully.

Welcome to Stop/Go!

The experiment consists of 3 matches. At the end of the 3 separate matches, you will be paid the total amount you have earned [TODO update with USD].

You will be assigned to be either the Red or the Blue player, and you will keep your role for all 3 matches.

Each match, you will be anonymously paired with a different player of the other role. You will never play again with the same player. Red moves first in the match, and has two choices: STOP or GO.

If the Red player chooses STOP, the match stops and both players receive a payoff of __ .

If the Red player chooses GO, it is the Blue player's turn to choose either LEFT or RIGHT. In this case, the payoff depends on what move the Blue player has made and on the state of the world, which is randomly chosen to be A or B (there is a 50/50 chance for either). Important! Only the Red player knows the state of the world before making the decision. The Blue player will be informed only after making a decision. The state of the world is randomly reselected each match.

If you understood the instructions correctly press the "Done" button to start a short tutorial of the game.
